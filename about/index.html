About

Myself and no less,
Proudly bear the battle scars,
I walk my own path.

It is a well known fact that all computer programmers are nerds or geeks, however, I can proudly say, I’m all of that and so much more. I’m 25 years old, I live in the Black Hills of South Dakota, and I’m a programmer, a gamer, a writer, and an animal lover.

I enjoy programming, it relaxes me, don’t ask why, I don’t know. I’m an OCD Bipolar who’s also got a touch of Asperger’s Syndrome, so, I guess I’m a bit socially awkward. Which is probably why I love computers so much, they are so much more logical than a human, and, they do whatever I tell them to…. usually.

By the way, that Haiku up above, yes that’s a Haiku, I wrote it, it’s not my greatest work ever, but it’ll do.
