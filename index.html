So, this post is here to explain my methodology for the categorizatoin and tagging of posts, namely the main differences between types of post.

Alright, categories will be in one of several types including intro[1], coding[2], gaming[3], writing[4], and other[5].

  1. An introductory post allowing me to add information about a variety of subjects. ↩

  2. A post about one of my code projects. ↩

  3. A post about games I play, especially tabletop games or Second Life. ↩

  4. A post about creative writing projects. ↩

  5. A post that does not fit into any other category. ↩
